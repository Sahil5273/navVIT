About Us

navVIT is an initiative designed to create a one-stop platform for all students at our college. We aim to simplify various college procedures, connect students with essential services, and enhance their overall experience.

Our Vision

We envision a campus where every student feels informed, connected, and supported. navVIT aspires to streamline navigation through college infrastructure, facilitate event discovery, and provide easy access to crucial information.

What We Offer

  • Efficient and organized information on hostel and day scholar facilities
  • Access to a comprehensive list of clubs and their activities
  • Up-to-date announcements on campus events and academic sessions
  • A centralized platform to search for frequently asked queries
